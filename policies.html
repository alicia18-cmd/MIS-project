Policies

Delivery Policy: As a company we offer two different types of shipping, standard shipping which is three to seven business days and express shipping which is one to three business days. Shipping charges will be calculated at checkout based on the delivery address and shipping type chosen. Orders over $50 will always get free standard shipping. Once your order has been shipped you will receive an e-mail with tracking details. If you do not receive your package please contact our customer service team at ACdresssupport@gmail.com And we will be happy to look into the issue.

Please note: Orders placed on Sundays or holidays will be processed the next business day so expect shipping to take a day longer.

Return Policy: As a company we accept returns within 30 days of the item being bought or delivered to your address period to be allowed to return the item it must be unused with tags and in its condition bought in. Sale or clearance items are not allowed to be returned. If you used cash to buy the item, you will receive the cash back immediately after returning the item and if you used card you will get a refund on your payment method used within 5 to 10 business days.

  • Credit cards, company credit cards will only be given to select employees and are only allowed to be used on company purchases
  • Cash handling, two people must count the cash at the end of each business day to risk theft or erroes dealing with cash
